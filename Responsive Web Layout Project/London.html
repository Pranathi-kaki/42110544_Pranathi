LONDON

London, the capital of England and the UK, is a city of rich history and modern charm. Located on the River Thames, it features landmarks like the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and the London Eye. Its cultural diversity and vibrant arts scene draw millions of visitors each year. Museums, theaters, and historic sites make it a global destination for tourism and heritage.


Beyond its attractions, London thrives as a center of finance, education, and innovation. Iconic neighborhoods like Camden and Mayfair offer unique experiences. The city is well-connected by its efficient transport network, including the famous Underground. London seamlessly blends its historic roots with contemporary energy.
