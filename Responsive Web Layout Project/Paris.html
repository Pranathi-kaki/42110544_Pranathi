PARIS

Paris, the capital of France, is renowned as the "City of Light" and a global hub for art, culture, and fashion. Nestled along the Seine River, it boasts iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum, home to masterpieces like the Mona Lisa. Known for its romantic ambiance, Paris is also celebrated for its charming streets, vibrant cafes, and exquisite cuisine, making it a dream destination for travelers worldwide.


Beyond its historical and architectural marvels, Paris is a modern metropolis and a center for innovation, business, and education. With its world-class galleries, theaters, and music venues, the city thrives as a cultural epicenter. Paris also hosts renowned events like Fashion Week and the French Open, further solidifying its reputation as a global city that seamlessly blends history, elegance, and contemporary charm.
